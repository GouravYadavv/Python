Namespace- it is a space that holds the names(identifiers)
		or
Namespace are dictionaries of identififers(keys) and their objects(values).

Their are 4 types of namespaces-
1. Local Namespace
2. Enclosing Namespace
3. Global Namespace
4. Built in Namespace


Decorators - They are the functions in python that receives another function as input and adds some functionality to it and returns it.

They are of 2 types:
1. Builtin decoratoes- @staticmethod, @classmethod, @abstractmethod and @property etc.
2. User defined decorators- that are defined by the user manually according to their needs.
